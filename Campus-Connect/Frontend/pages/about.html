🎓 About Campus Connect

Connecting India's Best Minds

A verified knowledge-sharing and mentorship platform bridging school aspirants, college students, and alumni from India's top engineering institutes.

What is Campus Connect?

More than a network — a verified community

Campus Connect is a platform built exclusively for verified students and alumni of IITs, NITs, IIITs, and IISc — and for school students who aspire to join them.

We believe the best guidance comes from people who have actually been there. A JEE aspirant should be able to talk to a real IITian. A first-year student should be able to reach a senior in their exact branch. An alumni should have a space to give back to the community that shaped them.

Campus Connect makes all of this possible — in one verified, safe, and trusted space.

The Problem We Solve

Why Campus Connect exists

Every year, lakhs of students face the same challenges — and there was no single trusted place to solve them.

😟

JEE Aspirants are Lost

School students preparing for JEE have no direct access to real IITians who can guide them with honest advice about preparation, branches, and college life.

🔍

No Verified Peer Network

College students can't easily find seniors or peers from other institutes in their exact branch for knowledge exchange, project collaboration, or career advice.

📵

Alumni Lose Touch

Alumni gradually lose connection with their institutes and juniors. There's no structured way for them to mentor the next generation or stay part of the community.

⚠️

Fake Profiles Everywhere

Existing social platforms are flooded with unverified profiles. Students can't trust whether the "IITian" they're talking to is actually from an IIT.

Our Core Values
